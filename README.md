# 👨‍💼 HR Analytics Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing employee data to understand workforce trends, attrition patterns, and key HR metrics. The dashboard provides insights that help organizations improve employee retention and make data-driven HR decisions.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI (Data Visualization)
- Excel (Data Cleaning & Preparation)
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

---

## 📊 Key Features
- KPI Cards: Total Employees, Attrition Rate, Average Salary, Average Age  
- Attrition Analysis by Department, Job Role, and Education  
- Employee Distribution by Age Group and Gender  
- Salary and Job Role Analysis  
- Interactive Filters (Department, Gender, Education Field)  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Identified departments with highest employee attrition  
- Analyzed impact of salary and job roles on attrition  
- Observed workforce distribution across age groups and education levels  
- Highlighted patterns that can help improve employee retention  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Visuals
- KPI Cards for quick insights  
- Bar Charts for attrition by department and job role  
- Pie/Donut Charts for employee distribution  
- Line/Column Charts for trend analysis  
- Slicers for interactive filtering  

---

## 🎯 Business Impact
This dashboard helps HR teams:
- Monitor employee attrition effectively  
- Identify problem areas in workforce management  
- Make informed decisions to improve employee satisfaction and retention  

---

## 📌 Conclusion
The HR Analytics Dashboard enables organizations to leverage data for strategic HR planning and improve overall workforce efficiency.
